Company add
 
To add a new company by click the add button in company  view screen
Enter the Company name, short name, legal name, street
Select the country,state,district,city
Enter the postal, area code, phone ,fax, email
Enter the website, register number,tngs number,cst number, tin number.
Enter service tax number, pan number,pf number.
The * mark fields are mandatory
The * mark fields are validation when submit this page
click the add button.
The company successfully added
