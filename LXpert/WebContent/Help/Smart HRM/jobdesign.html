Job Design
This is Job Design view screen. We can add, edit, and delete the Job Design
Make to add a new Job Design for click the add button
Make to edit Job Design for click the edit button
Want to delete the Job Design for click the delete button.
The view screen filtered by alphabetically and selection job grade
By default the job grade have a 3 groups
 
 
Job design Add :
 
You want to add a new job design , just click the add button in job design view screen
Enter the job title
Enter the job purpose
Enter the duties
Select authority’s person
Enter skill set & responsibility
Enter promotion & hazards
Select reporting to & department
Select test type and no of questions for each test type, passing percentage and time duration for test
Select the highest education.
Select from age and to age
Experience, height, weight, working time, gender
Enter scale, D.A & HRA
Select memory, creativity, Teaching ability, emotional stability
Select general skills & technical skills
Submit the add button
The job design successfully added.
