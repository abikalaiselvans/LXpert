This is allowance and recovery view screen. We can add, reset the allowance and recovery
To add a new allowance and recovery by click the add button
To reset allowance and recovery by click the reset button
To filter allowance and recovery by using alphabetic filter, office, search by id /name
 
 

Allowance and recovery add page
 
To add a allowance and recovery by click the add button in allowance and recovery view screen.

Click the Add button in allowance and recovery view screen.

Select the employee name, effective date.

Enter the allowance as hra and da,spl allowance, grade allowance etc
Enter the recovery as care trust, care it solution, petty advance etc

The * mark symbol are mandatory.
The * mark field validate when click the submit button
Click the submit button
The allowance and recovery successfully added.
