Onduty
This is  Onduty view screen. We can add,edit,delete the Onduty
To add a new Onduty by click the add button.
To edit a Onduty by click the edit button
To delete a Onduty  by click delete button.
To filtered Onduty  by alphabetically,month,year,date,staff,office.
 
 

Onduty Add
 
To add a new onduty by click the add button in onduty view screen.
Choose  the staffid.
Select the from date.
Choose the day.
Enter the place,reason.
Choose hours and minutes
Select authorized by.
The * mark symbol are mandatory
The * mark field validate when click the submit button
Click the submit button
The onduty successfully added.
