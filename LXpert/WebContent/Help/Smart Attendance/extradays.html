Extra days
This is  Extras day view screen. We can add, edit,delete the Extras day
To add a new Extras day by click the add button
To edit Extras day  by click the edit button
To filtered by  month, year,date,staff,office
 
 

Extras day Add
 
To add a new Extras day by click the add button in extras day view screen.
Choose  the Staff name
Select the date and day type.
Enter the description.
The * mark symbol are mandatory.
The * mark field validate when click the submit button
Click the submit button
The extras day successfully added.
 
 

Extras day Edit
 
Edit or change a extras day
Choose or tick the check box for extras day in view screen
Click the Edit button in extras day view screen.
Change the  date & day type.
Enter the description.
The * mark symbol are mandatory
The * mark field validate when click the submit button
Click the submit button.
The extras day successfully updated
