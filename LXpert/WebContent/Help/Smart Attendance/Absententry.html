Absent Entry
This is Continuous absent  entry view screen. We can add, edit, and delete the Continuous absent entry.
To add a new Continuous  absent  entry by click the add button
To edit the Continuous  absent  entry by click the edit button
To delete a Continuous absent entry  by click delete button.
To filtered Continuous entry by alphabetically,month,year,date,staff,office
 
 
Absent entry add
 
To add a new absent entry by click the add button in absent entry view screen
Choose  the staffid
Select the from and to date.
Choose day.
The * mark symbol are mandatory
The * mark field validate when click the submit button
Click the submit button
The absent entry successfully added.

Change the date,day type
The * mark symbol are mandatory
The * mark field validate when click the submit button
