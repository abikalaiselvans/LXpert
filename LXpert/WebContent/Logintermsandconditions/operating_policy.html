OPERATION POLICY
   
 

The Management is pleased to announce the operating policy for the year 2014. Also the Management proudly announces that previous year policy is well received and implemented at the most satisfaction of the Management as well as the Employees.
   
  The policy is designed in such a way that it benefits the Employee to fine our company as a dynamic and reward placing to work and the Management look forward to a productive and successful association. We consider the Employees of Mythra to be one of its most valuable resources. This manual has been written to serve as GUIDE for the EMPLOYEER EMPLOYEE RELATIONSHIPS.
   
 
This policy will be effective from 1st November 2014.
   
  SALARY :
   
Your position attracts a fixed salary group and the same will carry COMMON HRA/CONVEYANCE/SPECIAL ALLOWANCE and other benefits.
Except the BASIC SALARY the other benefits remains same for the fixed group.
   
  SALARY ADVANCE :
    The Management strongly discourage any salary advances.Its sole discretion of the management to grant or not to grant salary advance to any employee.
   
  PAY HIKE/INCREMENT:
    Based on your performance the PAY HIKE/INCREMENT will be given from time to time. The Management reserves the right to declare the increment as this is purely based on the performance and not on any other grounds.
    The increment will be calculated as follows.
    APPRAISAL RATING:
        SAE (Significantly Above Expectation)-positive increase of 17-20 percent in total compensation
        AE (Above Expectation)- positive increase of 12-16percent in total compensation
        ME (Meeting Expectation )- positive increase of 8-12 percent in total compensation
        NME (Not Meeting Expectation)- negative attraction which results in NO PAY REVISION SIX MONTHS TIME WILL BE GIVEN TO IMPROVE PERFORMANCE.
The Management reserves the right to terminate an employee who has received TWO CONSECUTIVE NMEs. PAY STRUCTURE REVISION will be scrutinized once in year or as and when needed.The above mentioned salary will be sacrosanct and will not be changed at any cost.
           
  TRAVEL POLICY
    TRAVEL :
      An overnight journey is considered as a TRAVEL
    TRAVEL ELIGIBILITY :
     

Any employee whose travel involves an overnight journey will be considered as TRAVEL and he/she will be eligible for the TRAVEL ALLOWANCE. All the employees of Mythra who has asked to take up travel by the Management with prior approval from the location head and immediate supervisor is eligible to TRAVEL
           
  TRAVEL ADVANCE :
   

The employee who has taken up travel can handle the same by getting Travel Advance from the PARENT LOCATION. No other out location is eligible to grant the TRAVEL ADVANCE to any employee.
    TRAVEL ADVANCE SETTLEMENT :
     

The TRAVEL ADVANCE should be settled within 7 working days time at the parent location. If not the forthcoming travel advances for the concerned employee will not be cleared by the Management.
     
NON SETTLEMENT OF TRAVEL ADVANCE MORE THAN 20 DAYS WILL RESULT IN THE DEDUCTION OF THAT AMOUNT FROM THE EMPLOYEES FORTHCOMING SALARY. THE MANAGEMENT WILL NOT BE LIABLE TO CLEAR THE TRAVEL BILLS SUBMITTED BEYOND 30 DAYS TIME.
           
  TRAVEL ALLOWANCE ELIGIBLITY
    Any employee whose travel involves an overnight journey will be considered as TRAVEL and he/she will be eligible for the TRAVEL ALLOWANCE.
   
All the employees of Mythra who has asked to take up travel by the Management with prior approval from the location head and immediate supervisor is eligible to TRAVEL.
   
Submitting all the bills is mandatory and the Management expects all the Employees strictly to adhere the same.
   
NON SUBMISSION OF BILLS WILL END INTO NON CLEARANCE OF ANY BILLS AT ANY COST. The Managing Director reserves the right of clearance in case of any deviation

  LOCAL TRAVEL:
    During travel the employee travels locally in the out locations is known as LOCAL TRAVEL. The respective local office should clear any local travel in the out locations.
    For example, An employee travelling from Madras to Madurai , the local travel of Madurai by the employee will be cleared only by the Madurai office .
    No other person from any other location including the Main/Registered Office Chennai will be eligible to clear the local travel allowance.
The Management expects travel only by AUTO /TWO WHEELER and NO CAR HIRING/TRAVEL is permitted. In exceptional cases the same can be done with prior approval from the Managing Director
    LOCAL CONVEYANCE:
     
All the local travel will be in TWO WHEELER, for which the reimbursement will be based on per KM charges. The charge per KM will be communicated from time to time.
     
Any employee travelling other than this mode (AUTO/BUS/CAR) has to get prior approval from the Managing Director. The same will be cleared on case to case
           
  OUT LOCATION CALL :
    Any travel which doesn't involve an overnight journey , but at the same time the travel distance exceeds 70 km is defined as out location call. The regular travel /conveyance policy doesn't apply to the out location calls.
    The Management follows the below mentioned policy on out location calls.
     
BREAK FAST : If you start before 7.00 am you are eligible to claim up to Rs.40/-
LUNCH : If you reach after 13.00 hrs you are eligible to claim up to Rs.50/-
DINNER : If you reach to parent location after 9.00 pm you are eligible to claim up to Rs.50/-
     
ONLY 50 PERCENT OF THE ABOVE MENTIONED AMOUNT WILL BE CLEARED IF THE EMPLOYEE DOESNT SUBMIT THE RELEVANT BILLS., WITH THE PROOF THAT HE/SHE HAS ATTENDED THE SPECIFIED CALL
   
  BILL CLEARNCE :
   
The travel bills will be cleared with immediate effect . The local travel bills will be cleared at locations after approval from the respective locations.
The actual amount will be cleared for travel from Residence to Bus /Railway Stations. THE MANAGEMENT EXPECTS ALL THE EMPLOYEES TO TAKE UP TRAVEL AS PER THE REGULAR /NORMAL NORMS AND MODE OF TRAVEL.
     
  LEAVE :
   
Till this time the Management was very liberal as far as taking up leaves. Now the Management express this is the right time to follow strict rules and regulations in this area.
    The Management expects all the employees to go through the below mentioned LEAVE POLICY
   
PRIVILEGE LEAVE : 12 DAYS (ONE DAY A MONTH )
SICK LEAVE : 3 DAYS
CASUAL LEAVE : 3 DAYS
    Maximum of 36 days leave accumulation will be allowed. Only Privilege Leave carry forwarded to the next financial year.
   
LEAVE DURING PROBATION WILL NOT BE ENTERTAINED AND WILL BE TREATED AS LOSS FOF PAY AFTER THE SICK LEAVE EXORTION.
   
Leave During Probation.
PRIVILEGE LEAVE : 2 DAYS
SICK LEAVE : 1 DAY
CASUAL LEAVE : 1 DAY
    COMPENSATION HOLIDAY:
     
Employees who works on Sundays/Declared Holidays will get compensatory holiday. The compensatory holiday will be declared only by the approval of divisional head. Also documentation for the same to the Human Resources Department is mandatory. It is the sole description of the management to grant compensatory holiday.
           
    PRIVILEGE LEAVE :
      The Leave which are earned in the previous year of an Employee can be enjoyed in the preceeding year.
       
    CASUAL LEAVE :
      Casual leave as mentioned above is only for 3 days and no employee should extend the leave .If an employee take casual leave more than a day that leave will be deducted from his privilege leave.
           
    SICK LEAVE :
      It is time off from work the workers can use to stay home to address the health and safety needs without losing pay.
      THE ABOVE MENTIONED LEAVE POLICY IS APPLICABLE ONLY FOR ONLINE EMPLOYEES. ITS PURELY THE DISCRTEATION OF THE MANAGEMENT AS FAR AS THE OUTSOURCED EMPLOYEES LEAVE STRUCTURE.
           
  LOSS OF PAY :
    Loss of pay,will be applicable to a confirmed/permanent employee if he avails more than 18 days leave after exhausting all the above mentioned categories.
    If an Employee on probation period avails more than 5 days leave after exhausting all the mentioned categories there will be loss of pay.
     
    EXAMPLES OF LOSS OF PAY :
     
If a confirmed/permanent employee takes 18days leave in a previous month (e.g. before July 31) and then takes 2 days leave in next month( august ,) there will be a deduction of 2 days salary as loss of pay.
     
If an employee in probation takes 5 day leave in a previous month(e.g. before July) and then takes 2 days leave in next month (august) there will be a deduction of 2 days salary as loss of pay
           
  MATERNITY :
    The Management strictly follows the conditions mentioned below for maternity leave.
   
  • The employee should not be on her probationary period.
   
  • Maximum leave will be for a period of 4 months.
   
  • One month leave will be taken into account as LEAVE WITH PAYMENT
   
  • The leave taken by the employee beyond 30 days will be taken as LEAVE WITH LOSS OF PAYMENT
    All the above benefits are only for employees who have not covered under ESI BENEFITS.
    THE EMPLOYEES COVERED UNDER ESI WILL GET THE BENEFITS AS PER THE RULES AND NORMS OF ESI.
           
  ILLNESS/ACCIDENTS
    For Long illness/Major accidents the Management will grant leave as per the following conditions.
   
  • The employee should not be in probationary period.
   
  • The employee will be granted leave for a maximum period of four months.
   
  • The employee will get leave upto 30 days as with salary.
   
  • More than 30 days leave will be considered as leave with out salary/ loss of pay
    THE ABOVE MENTIONED LEAVE PERIOD WILL NOT BE CONSIDERED/COVERED AS PART OF YOUR APPRAISAL CYCLE.
